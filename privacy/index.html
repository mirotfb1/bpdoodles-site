Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Basepoint Doodles is a personal project run by one person. This page explains what limited data is collected when you use this site and how it is handled.

What we collect

Email address — if you submit the waitlist form on the homepage, your email is stored by Formspree, a third-party form service. It is used only to notify you when email delivery becomes available. It will not be shared, sold, or used for any other purpose.

That is the only personal data collected. No accounts, no tracking pixels, no advertising.

Analytics

This site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is privacy-preserving and does not use cookies or fingerprinting. It collects aggregate data (page views, referrers, countries) with no personally identifiable information.

Cookies

This site does not set any cookies. Language preference (EN/BG) is stored in your browser's localStorage, which stays on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Third-party services

Formspree — processes waitlist form submissions. See Formspree's privacy policy.

Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's servers. See Google's privacy policy.

Cloudflare — the site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages and protected by Cloudflare's network. See Cloudflare's privacy policy.

Your rights

If you have submitted your email and want it removed, send a request to editor@bpdoodles.com and it will be deleted promptly.

Contact

Questions about this policy: editor@bpdoodles.com
